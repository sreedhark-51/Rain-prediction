📊 WHAT THE MODEL DOES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

INPUT: 7 environmental factors
  1. Rainfall (mm)
  2. River Level (m)
  3. Soil Moisture (%)
  4. Temperature (°C)
  5. Humidity (%)
  6. Elevation (m)
  7. Drainage Index (1-10)

OUTPUT: Flood Risk Prediction
  • Risk Level: Low / Moderate / High
  • Probability: 0-100%
  • Color-coded display
  • Descriptive message
